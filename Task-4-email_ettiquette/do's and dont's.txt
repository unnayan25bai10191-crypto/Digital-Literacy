Do’s
* Maintain a professional profile (especially on LinkedIn)
* Share accurate and meaningful content
* Think before posting anything online
* Respect others’ opinions and maintain digital etiquette
* Use strong passwords and enable security features
❌ Don’ts
* Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly
* Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
* Do not spread misinformation or fake news
* Avoid using abusive or informal language in professional spaces
* Do not copy others’ content without giving credit
