* In today’s digital world, communication plays a very important role in creating a professional impression. Many students, including myself, are used to informal communication through messaging apps, which often leads to poor email writing habits.
* For example, a student might send an email like “Send ASAP” without proper structure or politeness. This can create a negative impression, even if the intention is not wrong.
* Through this task, I understood that professional emails require a proper subject line, respectful tone, clear message, and appropriate closing. Good communication reflects seriousness, respect, and attention to detail.
* This task helped me realize that communication is not just about conveying information but also about how that information is presented.
