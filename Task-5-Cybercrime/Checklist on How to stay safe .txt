Stay Safe Online — Checklist
Always verify the sender’s email address carefully
Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
Never share passwords, OTPs, or personal credentials
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)
Check official websites before applying for opportunities
Avoid responding to messages that create urgency or panic
Keep your devices and software updated
Use strong and unique passwords for different accounts
Do not use public Wi-Fi for sensitive transactions
Regularly check account activity for unusual behavior

📢 Report cybercrime at:

National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in
Helpline: 1930
