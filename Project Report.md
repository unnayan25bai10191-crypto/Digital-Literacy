## Introduction

I completed this project as part of the Digital Literacy course. The goal was to develop essential digital skills needed in today’s academic and professional environments. As a Student Digital Ambassador, I help peers understand digital tools, maintain a professional online presence, and practice safe online behavior.

The project includes five tasks that cover topics like digital awareness, portfolio building, coding platforms, professional communication, and cybercrime awareness. Each task offered practical experience with commonly used digital tools and platforms. This portfolio reflects what I learned and how I applied digital literacy skills.

---

## Task 1 – Infographic

For this task, I created a digital literacy awareness infographic using Canva. The infographic emphasizes the importance of digital literacy, safe internet practices, and having a professional online presence.

It features key concepts, including useful digital tools like Google Docs and GitHub, along with tips for safe browsing and responsible online behavior. The design is simple and visually appealing for easy understanding.

One challenge I faced was organizing the information effectively in a limited space. This task, however, improved my ability to present information visually.

---

## Task 2 – Digital Portfolio

In this task, I created and updated profiles on platforms like GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. These platforms are important for building a professional online presence.

GitHub is useful for showcasing projects, LinkedIn is great for networking, and Kaggle helps in learning data science skills. I added my academic details and basic information to make my profiles look professional.

In the future, I plan to update these platforms regularly with my skills and projects to enhance my career opportunities.

---

## Task 3 – Platforms

In this task, I explored coding and collaboration platforms. I completed a beginner-level coding problem on HackerRank, which helped me grasp basic problem-solving.

I also created a Google Form quiz on digital literacy with five questions and linked it to a Google Sheet to collect responses.

These tools are very helpful for learning and collaborating. Coding platforms improve logical thinking, while Google Forms assist in conducting surveys and quizzes.

---

## Task 4 – Email Etiquette

This task focused on professional communication. I wrote two formal emails: one requesting an assignment extension and the other applying for an internship.

I learned the importance of proper subject lines, a polite tone, and structured content. Poor communication can lead to misunderstandings, so clear and professional communication is essential.

I also created a social media checklist to encourage responsible online behavior.

---

## Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness

In this task, I studied phishing as a common cybercrime. It involves tricking users into sharing sensitive information through fake messages or websites.

I learned how these attacks occur and their effects on individuals. It was surprising to see how realistic these scams can be.

One habit I plan to change is being more cautious before clicking links or sharing information online.

---

## Conclusion

This project helped me understand the importance of digital literacy in academic and professional life. It enhanced my knowledge of online tools, communication, and cybersecurity.

Overall, the project was a valuable learning experience that will benefit me in the future.
